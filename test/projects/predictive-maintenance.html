Predictive Maintenance System

Overview

The Predictive Maintenance System revolutionizes industrial efficiency through advanced hardware and software integration. Designed for harsh environments, it ensures seamless performance in industries like mining, automotive, and food production. By leveraging real-time monitoring and predictive analytics, this system minimizes downtime and enhances productivity.

Key Features

  • Real-time rotor vibration monitoring for early detection.
  • Predictive analytics offering a six-month failure forecast.
  • Multi-mode communication: 4G, LoRaWAN, BLE, and Wi-Fi.
  • Augmented Reality (AR) for remote troubleshooting.
  • Digital twin integration for enhanced diagnostics and simulations.

Impact

This system has achieved significant success, increasing profitability by over 25% and reducing unplanned downtime. Its advanced analytics and AR-enhanced troubleshooting provide a cutting-edge solution to industrial challenges.
