Small Size League (SSL) Robots

The Small Size League (SSL) Robots were designed and developed at the Parsian Robotics Center to compete in international and national robotics competitions. These robots demonstrate cutting-edge innovations in robotics hardware and software, focusing on speed, precision, and coordinated actions.

Key Features

  • Advanced hardware design optimized for high-speed performance and durability.
  • Artificial intelligence implementation for strategic decision-making and real-time adaptability.
  • Intricate path planning algorithms for smooth and efficient navigation.
  • Seamless communication systems enabling synchronized actions among robots.

Notable Achievements

Title Event
4th Place in the Small Size League RoboCup 2017, Nagoya, Japan
3rd Place in the Small Size League IranOpen 2017

The SSL Robots are a testament to the commitment to excellence and innovation at the Parsian Robotics Center. They serve as a benchmark for high-performance robotics in competitive environments.
