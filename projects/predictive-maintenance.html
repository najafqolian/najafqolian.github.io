Predictive Maintenance System

Overview

The Predictive Maintenance System revolutionizes industrial efficiency by combining real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and augmented reality. Designed for harsh industrial environments, it reduces downtime, optimizes maintenance schedules, and enhances profitability across sectors like mining, automotive, and food production.

Key Features

  • Real-time rotor vibration monitoring for early detection.
  • Failure prediction with six months' lead time.
  • Multi-mode communication (4G, LoRaWAN, BLE, and Wi-Fi).
  • Augmented Reality (AR) for enhanced troubleshooting.
  • Digital twin technology for simulations and diagnostics.

Impact

This cutting-edge system has increased industrial profitability by over 25% while minimizing unplanned downtime. Its robust design and advanced features ensure seamless operation in the most demanding environments.
