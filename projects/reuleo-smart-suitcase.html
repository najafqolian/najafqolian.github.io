Reuleo Smart Suitcase

Overview

The Reuleo Smart Suitcase is an innovative and user-centric mobility solution designed to transform the way people travel and manage their belongings. Developed with cutting-edge technology, it features advanced robotics, intelligent navigation, and versatile functionality, making it a leader in the smart suitcase market. The suitcase combines sleek design with practical usability, catering to both individual travelers and professionals. Its groundbreaking features have earned recognition at international competitions and among industry leaders.

Key Features

  • Smart Mobility: Equipped with motorized omni-wheels for smooth navigation across various terrains.
  • Autonomous Navigation: Follows its owner using advanced 3D image processing and proximity sensors.
  • Versatile Design: Can transform into a scooter for added functionality and convenience.
  • Enhanced Security: Features alarm systems, automatic locking, GPS tracking, and 3G/4G connectivity for global monitoring.
  • Integrated Features: Includes automatic luggage weighing, a 10,000 mAh battery for device charging, and smartphone connectivity.
  • Companion App: Provides seamless logistics management, control, and real-time updates.

Impact

  • Recognized Excellence: Awarded top honors at Germany's Start Up World competition for innovation and usability.

  • Broad Applicability: Appeals to diverse user groups, from frequent travelers to professionals seeking advanced mobility solutions.
