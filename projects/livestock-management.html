Livestock Management System

Overview

The Livestock Management System is an innovative solution designed to transform the agriculture and husbandry sectors by integrating state-of-the-art technology for efficient and humane livestock monitoring and tracking. With a proven track record across 13 industry-specific projects, this system offers comprehensive monitoring capabilities tailored to the unique needs of livestock management. Deployed in major organizations, it enhances operational efficiency and animal welfare while maintaining flexibility for diverse applications.

Key Features

  • Smart Gateway: Monitors critical parameters such as weight, commuting, age, vaccination status, and breed for informed decision-making.
  • Advanced Tag Technology: Features passive and active underskin or bolus tags capable of tracking ID, temperature, pH, location, and estrus cycles.
  • Durable Design: Includes tags like Thermo-Motion Tags Plus with a 5m range and boluses with a 4-year battery life, ensuring longevity and reliability.
  • Comprehensive Monitoring: Offers real-time tracking and health monitoring to optimize livestock management.
  • Versatility: Designed for integration with existing systems for seamless deployment and scalability.

Impact

  • Enhanced Operational Efficiency: Streamlines livestock management processes, saving time and resources.
  • Improved Animal Welfare: Enables proactive health monitoring and care, reducing stress and ensuring better living conditions.
  • Sustainability: Supports long-term resource management with minimal waste and extended asset lifespan.
  • Cross-Industry Applications: Successfully deployed in agriculture and husbandry sectors, highlighting its adaptability.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Provides actionable insights for improved decision-making and productivity.
