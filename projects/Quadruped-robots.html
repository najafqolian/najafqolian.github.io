Quadruped Robot

Overview

This four-legged robot was developed as an undergraduate project with a self-funded budget, focusing on achieving fundamental walking and balancing capabilities. The project emphasizes simplicity and functionality, utilizing basic components to explore the core dynamics of robotic locomotion. Designed to balance and walk effectively, this robot serves as an entry point into understanding complex gait algorithms and hardware integration, showcasing the potential of affordable and accessible robotics development.
