About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am an engineer, researcher, and innovator with expertise in robotics, mechatronics, and advanced control systems. With a Master’s degree in Mechatronics from the University of Tehran and a proven track record in cutting-edge research, I specialize in developing intelligent solutions for real-world challenges. My projects range from predictive maintenance systems to multi-robot formation control, blending AI, machine learning, and hardware design.
